Our exhibitions

Te Papa is New Zealand's bold and innovative national museum and a recognised world leader in interactive and visitor-focused museum experiences. New Zealand's geology and natural environment, the stories of New Zealand's indigenous people, and the art of New Zealand are all explored in Te Papa's long-term exhibitions.
